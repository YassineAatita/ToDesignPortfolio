Detail

Our design features a unique and imaginative concept of a taxi flying through space with the earth visible in the background. The taxi is depicted in great detail, The taxi is also shown with wings, engines and other features that suggest it has been retrofitted for space travel. The space background adds a futuristic and otherworldly element to the design, with stars and galaxies scattered throughout the image. The earth in the background is depicted in a realistic way, with the blue oceans and green continents visible. This creates a sense of contrast between the familiar world of earth and the unknown and exciting space environment.
