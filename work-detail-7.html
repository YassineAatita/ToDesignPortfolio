FLASHMARKET LOGO

FLASHMARKET LOGO

Client

FlashMarket COMPNY

Year

2021

Service

Logo Design

UI/UX

Photoshop/illustrator

Detail

The FlashMarket logo is a vibrant and eye-catching design that combines three distinct elements to represent the brand. The letter "F" and "M" are integrated into the logo, representing the name of the store, while a striking flash icon adds an element of speed and energy to the design. The use of yellow and orange colors in the logo adds to the sense of vibrancy and excitement, making it instantly recognizable and memorable. Overall, the FlashMarket logo successfully conveys the brand's focus on speed and efficiency, while also projecting a sense of warmth and friendliness that appeals to local customers.
